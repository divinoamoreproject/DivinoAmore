About Us

Divino Amore is a journey of faith and art born from the desire to bring the Gospel to the world through dance, art and solidarity.

Who we are

We are not just a group of dancers: we are people who want to place their talent into God’s hands so that it may become service, listening and light for those who are watching.

Each person who joins this path becomes part of a choreography, but also part of a story, a life that is welcomed and cherished.

The heart of the project

The project is led by Simone Giovanni Messina, who felt deep inside the desire to unite his passion for God with his artistic calling.

Around him a group of dancers and collaborators gathered: people who share the same desire to announce the Gospel through beauty, creativity and concrete love for those in need.

Our mission

Through performances, outreach and gestures of solidarity, Divino Amore wants to remind every person that they are loved and that their story is precious. We carry this message into parishes, theatres, squares and wherever there is a heart willing to listen.
