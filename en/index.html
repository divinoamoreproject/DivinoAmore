DIVINO AMORE PROJECT

The Gospel through dance, art and solidarity.

What is Divino Amore?

Divino Amore is a faith-based dance project born in Rome, Italy. We bring the message of the Gospel into streets, churches and theatres through contemporary dance, live music and powerful testimonies.

Faith in motion

Every choreography is inspired by the Bible and by real stories of grace, healing and inner freedom.

Art that serves

We use dance and the performing arts to create spaces of listening, beauty and encounter for all generations.

Love in action

Part of the project is dedicated to concrete solidarity: supporting people in need through offerings and charity actions.

If you would like to host Divino Amore in your parish, city or event, you can contact us through the Italian contact page or by WhatsApp.
