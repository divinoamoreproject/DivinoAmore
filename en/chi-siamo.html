About Us

Divino Amore is a journey of faith and art born from the desire to bring the Gospel into the world through dance, art, and solidarity.

We are not just a group of dancers: we are people who try to place our gifts in God's hands so they may become service, listening, and light for those who watch.

The Heart of the Project

The project is led by Simone Giovanni Messina, who deeply felt the call to unite his passion for dance with the Word of God. Around this calling, dancers came together with the same desire: to proclaim Love with the body, presence, and care.
